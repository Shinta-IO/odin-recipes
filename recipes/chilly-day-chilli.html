Chilly Day Chilli

Warm up with a family favorite on a cold day!

Whip up a hearty and flavorful Ground Chicken Chili that’s perfect for cozy dinners or casual gatherings. This recipe starts with ground chicken browned in vegetable oil, paired with tender pinto beans, sweet onions, and a duo of diced and Mexican-style stewed tomatoes for a zesty kick. A blend of chili powder, brown sugar, vinegar, mustard, and a hint of black pepper infuses the dish with a smoky-sweet tang and just the right amount of spice. Simmered to perfection with a splash of water to meld the flavors, this rich and robust chili is a wholesome, satisfying meal that’s sure to warm your soul.

Ingredients

  • 1 TBSP Vegtable Oil
  • 2 Lbs Ground chicken
  • 1 Can (15 Ounces) Pinto Beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 Can (14.5 Ounces) Diced Tomatoes with Juice
  • 2 Onions, halved thinly sliced
  • 1 Can (14.5 Ounces) Mexican Style stewed Tomatoes with Chiles, undrained
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 2 TBSP Brown Sugar
  • 2 TBSP Chilli Powder
  • 2 TBSP vinegar
  • 2 TBSP prepared mustard
  • 2 TSP Salt
  • Black Pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a soup pot over medium heat. Cook and stir ground chicken in the hot oil until no longer pink and the chicken is crumbly, about 10 minutes. Drain.
  2. Stir in pinto beans, onions, diced tomatoes, Mexican-style stewed tomatoes, water, brown sugar, chili powder, vinegar, mustard, salt, and black pepper; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer chili until thickened and flavors have blended, about 3 hours. Stir occasionally and add extra water if needed.
